---
title: What is LearnRep
description: Generate quizzes from anything, anywhere — terminal, IDE, or app.
---

LearnRep turns any content into a quiz. Paste a topic, pipe in a file, or use it from your IDE — and get a shareable, scored quiz in seconds.

It works three ways:

- **CLI** — `lr generate "react hooks"` from your terminal
- **MCP** — call it from Claude Code, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible app
- **Web** — browse, take, and share quizzes at [learnrep.ai](https://learnrep.ai)

Quizzes support four question types: multiple choice, multi-select, open-ended, and code-writing. Open-ended and code answers are graded by AI.

LearnRep is open source. The CLI and core library are MIT licensed.

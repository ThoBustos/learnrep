---
title: lr stats
description: Print your learning stats to the terminal.
---

## Usage

```bash
lr stats
```

## Output

```
Streak:          12 days
Topics mastered: 4
Avg improvement: +14%
Quizzes taken:   23
```

**Streak** — consecutive days with at least one completed quiz attempt.

**Topics mastered** — topics where you have scored 80% or above on at least one attempt.

**Avg improvement** — average of (latest score minus first score) across quizzes with two or more attempts.

**Quizzes taken** — number of distinct quizzes attempted, not total attempt count.
